WELCOME TO INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

 
 Institute of Science & Technology is a self financed college, sponsored by Gopsai Avinandan Sangha ( A Society registered under West Bengal Societies Act XVII, 1961)
 has been started in the year 2005 with a noble aim of imparting technical education to the rural masses in and around West Bengal.
 The institution enables them to be placed as the best professionals in industries and make them enter into high level programmes wicompetence and confidence.
 The College is offering ITI, Diploma (Polytechnic), B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, BCA, BBA, BHM & MHA programme under a single umbrella. TCampus of the college is very clean, green 
 and beautiful providing an excellent ambience for learning. The college is recognized Department of Higher Education, Govt. of West Bengal and Approved by the AICTE, New Delhi 
 and Affiliated to MAKAUT, WB for B.Tech...
